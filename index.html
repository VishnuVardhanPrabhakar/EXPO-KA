Our Story

Started as a small family business in Kolar district, ShivNanadi Dairy Farm has been serving the community with fresh, high-quality dairy products since 2020. As a unit of VARDHA, we maintain the highest standards of animal care and product quality.

We are committed to providing fresh, organic milk and cow products directly from our farm to your doorstep, following traditional farming methods that have been passed down through generations.
