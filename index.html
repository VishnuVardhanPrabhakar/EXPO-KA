A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1890 by the Blackwood family, Heritage Dairy Farm has been a cornerstone of quality dairy production for over 135 years. What began as a small family operation with just twelve cows has grown into a respected institution, yet we've never forgotten our roots.

Our historic stone barn, built in 1892, still stands today as a testament to our enduring commitment to traditional methods. Every morning, our cows graze on the same rolling pastures their ancestors did, producing milk of extraordinary quality.

We believe in honoring the past while embracing responsible stewardship. Our products are crafted using recipes perfected over five generations, ensuring that every bite and sip connects you to a rich heritage of excellence.
